SRAD Solid Propulsion Guide

Welcome to the UTSA Aeronautics and Rocketry Propulsion handbook. This site serves as the primary technical reference for designing, casting, and testing single-stage solid motors.

⚠️ SAFETY FIRST: No propellant manufacturing or testing is to be conducted without a Level 2 certified member present and a cleared safety plan.

BATES Grain Design

For our SRAD motors, we typically utilize BATES (Ballistic Test Evaluation System) grains. These allow for a neutral burn surface area as the outer diameter decreases while the inner core and ends increase.

  • Core Diameter: Critical for initial Kn calculation.
  • Inhibitor: Ensure the outer diameter is properly inhibited to prevent casing burns.

Static Fire Procedures

Success is measured in data. All motors must undergo a static fire to characterize the burn rate exponent (n) and temperature coefficient (a).
